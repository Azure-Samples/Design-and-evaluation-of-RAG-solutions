QuickConnect Customer Service Documentation

Handling Lost or Stolen Equipment

As a customer service representative at QuickConnect, you may encounter situations where customers report their telecommunications equipment, such as modems, routers, or mobile devices, as lost or stolen. This document is designed to help you navigate these scenarios efficiently and provide excellent support to our customers.

Steps to Follow When Handling Lost or Stolen Equipment Cases

  1. Verify Customer Identity

    Before proceeding with any actions, ensure the authenticity of the customer by asking for identifiable information, such as:

    • Full Name
    • Account Number
    • Address on File
    • Last 4 Digits of Social Security Number (SSN)
    • Security Questions (if set up)

  2. Document the Incident

    It’s vital to keep accurate records of the reported incident. Use our CRM system to log the details, including:

    • Date and time of report
    • Description of the equipment
    • Serial number or identification code of the equipment (if available)
    • Circumstances of the loss/theft

  3. Deactivate the Equipment

    To prevent unauthorized use, deactivate the lost or stolen equipment immediately:

    1. Access the equipment management system in the CRM.
    2. Locate the specific equipment using the serial number or account number.
    3. Change the equipment status to ‘Lost/Stolen.’

  4. Provide Replacement Options

    Inform the customer of their options for replacing the lost or stolen equipment:

    • Offer to purchase a new device through QuickConnect.
    • Provide information about approved third-party retailers.
    • Look up any available insurance or warranty claim information.

  5. Advise on Security Measures

    Help the customer avoid future incidents by advising them on safety measures:

    • Keep equipment in secure locations when not in use.
    • Use security features like passwords, PIN codes, or encryption.
    • Report any suspicious activity immediately.

QuickConnect Equipment Replacement Policies

Below is a summary of our replacement policies related to lost or stolen equipment:

Equipment Type Replacement Policy
Routers Customers can purchase a replacement router from QuickConnect or an approved retailer. Limited discounts may be available for loyal customers.
Modems Replacement modems can be purchased through our online store or in select retail locations. Device protection plans cover 50% of the cost if enrolled.
Mobile Devices QuickConnect offers a range of mobile devices. Customers with insurance plans can file a claim for a replacement. Otherwise, they must purchase a new device.

Important Notes

Important: Ensure all actions taken are documented in the customer’s account notes. Clear communication and accurate records are essential for providing consistent and high-quality customer service.

FAQs for Handling Lost or Stolen Equipment

  1. What should customers do if they find their lost equipment?

    Advise customers to contact QuickConnect customer service immediately. We will reactivate the equipment, ensuring it is secure and properly functioning. The customer must verify their identity before reactivation.

  2. Can customers track their stolen equipment?

    Some mobile devices and routers have built-in tracking capabilities. Direct customers to the device manufacturer's support for detailed tracking instructions. However, QuickConnect cannot track or locate lost/stolen equipment.

  3. Are there any charges for deactivating lost or stolen equipment?

    QuickConnect does not charge a fee for deactivating equipment that has been reported lost or stolen. Some replacement options may involve costs depending on the customer’s insurance plan and account status.
