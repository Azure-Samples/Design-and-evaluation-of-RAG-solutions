1. Understanding Customer Needs

First and foremost, it’s crucial to understand the specific requirements and preferences of the customer. Follow these steps to accurately gauge their needs:

  • Start by asking open-ended questions such as:
    • "What feature or service are you currently missing?"
    • "Why are you considering an upgrade at this time?"
  • Listen actively and take notes on the customer’s key points.
  • Acknowledge their concerns and restate their needs to ensure clarity.

3. Eligibility Check

Before proceeding with a service upgrade, confirm the customer’s eligibility by evaluating the following:

  • Check if the customer’s existing equipment supports the higher service tier. If not, advise them on necessary upgrades.
  • Review the customer’s current billing status. Ensure there are no overdue balances.
  • Confirm if the upgrade is available at the customer’s geographic location. Some upgrades might be restricted in certain areas.

4. Procedure for Handling Upgrade Requests

Follow these steps when processing a service upgrade request:

  1. Access the Customer Account:
    • Log in to the QuickConnect customer management system.
    • Locate the customer’s account using their account number or registered phone number.
  2. Review Current Services:
    • Check the customer’s existing services and usage patterns.
    • Provide recommendations based on their current plan and needs.
  3. Select Upgrade:
    • Discuss available upgrade options with the customer.
    • Send them a detailed comparison of current and potential upgrade plans via email if requested.
    • Confirm the customer’s choice.
  4. Process the Upgrade:
    • Select the chosen upgrade option in the system.
    • Inform the customer of any additional equipment requirements or installation appointments.
    • Ensure to cover any changes in billing, including prorated charges and future monthly billing adjustments.
  5. Confirm Changes:
    • Recap all changes and improvements the customer will experience with the new plan.
    • Inform the customer about the timeline and any actions required on their part.
  6. Complete Documentation:
    • Document the service upgrade request in the system.
    • Note any customer queries and follow-up actions required.
  7. Follow-Up:
    • Schedule a follow-up call or email to ensure the service upgrade was implemented to the customer's satisfaction.

5. Common Questions

Here are answers to some frequently asked questions by customers concerning service upgrades:

  1. How long does it take for the upgrade to be activated?

    The activation time varies depending on the service plan. Typically, it can take anywhere from a few hours to 2 business days.

  2. Will my internet speed be affected during the upgrade process?

    There may be a brief disruption during the transition, but it should not last longer than a few minutes. We recommend performing any upgrades during off-peak hours.

  3. How does the billing work for upgrades?

    The billing adjustment for the upgraded plan will be prorated for the current month. Subsequently, the regular monthly rate for the new plan will apply.

  4. Do I need new equipment for the upgraded service?

    In some cases, new equipment may be required. Our support team will inform you of any necessary upgrades and provide assistance in acquiring new equipment.
